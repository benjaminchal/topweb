The Odin Project

What is The Odin Project?

The Odin Project is a high altitude ballooning project whose aim is to launch a high altitude balloon into the atmosphere. From here we hope to take beautiful photographs of the earth and conduct meteorological experiments/observations.

UPDATES: We are currently working on the radio beacon to locate the balloon whilst it is in the air
